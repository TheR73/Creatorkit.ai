How to use the Tag Generator

  1. Enter a keyword related to your content (e.g., "fitness", "travel")
  2. Select your target platform (optional)
  3. Click "Generate Tags" to get relevant hashtags
  4. Copy the hashtags and use them in your posts
  5. Click "Generate More" for additional suggestions

Pro Tip

Use a mix of popular (high competition) and niche (low competition) hashtags for better reach. Aim for 5-10 relevant hashtags per post.
