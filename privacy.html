Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2023

1. Information We Collect

CreatorKit.AI is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require accounts or collect personal information to use our tools.

We may collect:

  • Anonymous usage data through Plausible Analytics (no cookies)
  • Information you voluntarily submit through our tools (e.g., text for voiceovers)
  • Browser information for compatibility purposes

We do not store your input data permanently. All processing happens in your browser unless otherwise noted.

2. Advertising

To keep CreatorKit.AI free, we show ads through third-party networks like Adsterra. These ads may use cookies or similar technologies to personalize content.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

3. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to:

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Track tool usage limits (e.g., subtitle generation limits)

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
